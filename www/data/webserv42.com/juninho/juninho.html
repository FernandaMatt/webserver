Juninho - The Rubber Duck

In programming, a rubber duck is a method of debugging code. The idea is that by explaining your code, line by line, to an inanimate object like a rubber duck, you might identify errors or understand the problem better.

Juninho is our rubber duck mascot at 42, symbolizing the importance of clear thinking and communication in solving programming challenges.

And we would like to thank Juninho and all the ohter cadets that served as our ruber ducks throught the 42 journey.
